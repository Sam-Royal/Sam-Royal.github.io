PLASMA PSY

Blitz’ Psy gives him several powers. Including unmatched speed, the ability to summon a sword, the ability to stick to surfaces through electromagnetism, enhanced reflexes and reaction speeds, and Telekinesis.
It also gives him complete control over the elements of Electricity, Fire, and general plasma.

PERSONALITY

Blitz is an extremely optimistic person. Always seeing the light at the end of the tunnel, and the spark of hope within every person he meets. Because of this, he refuses to end the life of anyone. He's extremely extroverted, and generally good with people in contrast to his sister, Skyler. Despite this, he's not very popular.

Blitz is extremely determined, and will do everything in his power to realize a goal that he's set his mind on. He can be quite impatient and reckless, and those around him typically see this optimism as naivety more than anything.

He never abandons his integrity, And always remains true to himself, and his values. Even if the deviate from others.

CREATION

Creation’s ability allows him to create anything he can imagine with the only caveat being that regardless of what he creates, it will always shatter when hit with the slightest amount of resistance.

PERSONALITY

Creation has lost all hope in humanity, and doesn’t consider himself one in the slightest. He’s fine with anything else, Angel, Monster, Demon, Devil, but a person is the worst thing he could possibly be. In his eyes people are the root of the world’s flaws, and only exist for the sole purpose of perpetuating the suffering of the other people. He finds himself constantly berating anyone he deems human for, not only their flaws, but the flaws they associate themselves with by being human, always in a sarcastic manner and a faked lighthearted tone.

Creation will not let go of the past or his own, and bears fatal wounds and an oxygen-insulating mask to prove it.

BIO

Despite never letting go of the past, he never shares any aspect of it, and everything known about him could just be a lie he created to prove a point.

All we know about him is that something happened; something terrible to the point that he no longer wants to be human.
