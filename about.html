Legends Unleashed is a hectic combonation between a 3D battle game and an open world adventure game. With 5 seperate stories each with a different main fighter and a different perspective on the over-arching storyline. Our game, along with those 5 fighters has around 20 others for you to play as, and an online battle mode. Each of our characters have been fleshed out in order to avoid stereotype heroes, or at the very least, poke fun at them. Although we cannot say the same about the villains, at least their abilites are somewhat unique!
